Type Racing Game

How to Play

CREATING A ROOM

(1) Click on "Create a private a room"

(2) Share the room name displayed on the top left

(3) Wait for the timer at the top to count down

(4) First to complete the provided text wins!

JOINING A ROOM

(1) Click on "Join a room"

(2) Type the room name

(3) Click on "enter"

(3) Wait for the timer at the top to count down

(4) First to complete the provided text wins!
